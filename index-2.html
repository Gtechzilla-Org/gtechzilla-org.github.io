Century Foundations

Century Trading Company is a trusted global provider of mineral trading & scrap metals, food & cereals, and cargo services. With over 15 years of experience, we connect industries and communities worldwide with quality products and seamless logistics — ensuring smooth operations from source to destination. We are based in Kenya, with offices in Nairobi, Mombasa, Eldoret, Dar es Salaam, and Kampala, serving clients across Africa and international markets.

Our expertise spans mineral trading & scrap metals for industrial and commercial applications, food & cereals supply to nourish communities, and cargo services that move goods securely and efficiently across the globe. We proudly serve regional and international clients, meeting the unique needs of diverse markets with professionalism and integrity.
