Our Commitment

Mineral Trading & Scrap Metal Management

At Century Trading Company, we are dedicated to delivering excellence in mineral trading and scrap metal management. We connect global buyers and sellers of precious minerals and metals, while promoting responsible recycling of scrap materials for a cleaner planet.

Our Offerings

With our deep industry knowledge and trusted network, we ensure quality, authenticity, and timely delivery of all our products and services:

  • Gold Bars – Supplying high-purity gold bars for investment and industrial use
  • Diamond Trading – Ethical sourcing and distribution of rough and polished diamonds
  • Metal Trading – Providing various industrial metals for manufacturing and construction
  • Copper Cathode – Delivering premium-grade copper cathodes to meet diverse needs
  • Scrap Metal – Collection, processing, and recycling of scrap metals responsibly
  • Gold Nuggets – Offering natural gold nuggets for collectors and jewelers

Our team works closely with our clients to understand their requirements and deliver customized solutions, while upholding the highest ethical and environmental standards in all our operations.
