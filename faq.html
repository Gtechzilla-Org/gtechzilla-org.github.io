FAQ'S

At Century trading Company , our specialty is working with our clients to understand their business and supply chain needs. But it is also important for our prospective clients to understand what we do and how we do it. This helps to identify the areas where our expertise will benefit your company.

See below some answers that may help you. However, we are always available to answer any further questions you may have. Contact us anytime for a free consultation. Here are some answers to many questions about our Industry as well as our services, expertise and company structure

WHAT IS LOGISTICS MANAGEMENT?

Logistics management is a function where a company usually consisting of various shipping and distribution professionals provide services to analyze a company’s supply chain, offer carrier and service options, and provide continued management of all or some areas of a customer’s transportation needs.

DOES IT COST ANYTHING TO DO A FULL ANALYSIS OF OUR SUPPLY CHAIN SERVICES?

No, we do not charge for our initial analysis. There is absolutely nothing to lose by having our expert logistics professionals review your shipping and distribution spend and profile. In most cases we are compensated by our providers should you utilize our services.

WHAT MODES AND SERVICES DOES Century trading Company PROVIDE?

We offer services for international and domestic freight, small parcel shipping, B 2 C solutions, freight and parcel audit and reporting, and overall supply chain management.

I AM A SMALL START-UP, CAN Century trading Company HELP ME?

Yes, we love working with small and emerging companies. Century trading Company provides the expertise and support to help your company scale.

WHAT TECHNOLOGY CAN Century trading Company PROVIDE US?

We offer top-notch technology solutions to handle your freight, small parcel, and E-commerce needs. Each system gives you full visibility and the tools to best manage your shipping. Request a demo so we can show you how easy it is.

DOES Century trading Company HAVE INTERNATIONAL CAPABILITIES?

Yes, Century trading Company Limited is a licensed agent of UnitedEx, a leading authorized reseller of DHL Express services. In addition, we offer the services of valued freight and brokerage service providers, giving our customers options for ocean and air freight, for any import or export needs.
